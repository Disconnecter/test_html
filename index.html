AltStore Quick Start

Download AltStore PAL and add the Anne Ferret source

This page summarizes the current installation flow from altstore.io/download and gives you a one-tap button for adding the custom source in AltStore.

Open Official Download Page Open NotifyMe in AltStore

How to install AltStore PAL

  1. Tap the official download button and allow the Marketplace Installation prompt from AltStore LLC.
  2. Open the Settings app and allow marketplace access for AltStore LLC under your Apple ID section.
  3. Return to the official download page, tap download again, and confirm the App Marketplace installation.

Requirements from the official page

  • Use Safari, Chrome, or Vivaldi on iPhone or iPad.
  • Your device needs to be physically located in the European Union or Japan.
  • You must be signed in to an EU or Japanese App Store account.
  • Your device must be running iOS or iPadOS 18.0 or newer.

Custom source button

The image button above opens this link: altstore://source/?url=https://altstore.anneferret.eu/source.json.

It will only work on a device where AltStore is already installed and the altstore:// URL scheme is recognized.
